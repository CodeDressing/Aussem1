Build the world's most intelligent residential real estate platform.

Aussem1 is designed to become the single destination for understanding any residential property through artificial intelligence, public records, market intelligence, comparable sales, predictive analytics, and future MLS integration.

Every property begins with one address.

Aussem1 is not a listing website.
