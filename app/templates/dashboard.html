Source Governance Rules

Aussem1 must be explicit about what the platform knows, what it does not know, and which source type is required for each real estate claim.

Strict Source Mode

Public Records Can Support

Parcel identity, block/lot, tax assessment, property tax context, deed references, mortgage references, lien references, sale-history references, owner references, GIS context, MOD-IV context, municipality, county, state, and building facts where source-backed.

Public Records Cannot Prove Alone

Active listing status, under-contract status, pending status, current listing price, days on market, showing availability, offer deadline, broker confirmation, or current MLS status.

Future Authorized Feeds Required

MLS / RESO, IDX, broker-authorized listing feeds, valuation models, comparable datasets, school APIs, flood/risk layers, and persistence will be connected as separate source-governed systems.
